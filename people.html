Dr. Mark I. Richardson | mir@aeolisresearch.com
Scientist
Mark Richardson was educated at Imperial College London and UCLA, and was an Assistant Professor of Planetary Science at Caltech from 2001-2010. He is a Co-Investigator on the Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter Mars Climate Sounder and the Mars Science Laboratory (Curiosity) Rover Environmental Monitoring Station (REMS), and a Participating Scientist on the Mars Odyssey THEMIS.
